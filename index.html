About Me


My name is Gregg DiChiara. When I began this project, I wasn't sure how to go about it. When I tried to select an image from the LoremPixel site, it took forever for any of the images to load. It then occurred to me that maybe I could use one of my own stock images. The image to the left is my cat Cinder. Cinder and I were great "mousers". Cinder has a great nose and he can sniff out a mouse from anywhere. But when it comes to actually getting rid of the mouse, well, he needs some help. That's where I come in. I get down and follow Cinder's cues. Then I try to outsmart the mouse by figuring out escape routes. Early on, I made the mistake of underestimating the mouse. I have since come to learn That mice are imbued with X-Men type powers. So you could think of this story as "Of X-Men and X-Mice".
